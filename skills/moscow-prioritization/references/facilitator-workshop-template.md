# MoSCoW Workshop Template

## Inputs

- Candidate requirements list
- Release date and budget
- Delivery team capacity
- Decision owners

## Agenda (60 minutes)

1. Review objective and release constraints (10 min)
2. Categorize top requirements using decision tree (30 min)
3. Challenge all `Must` candidates (10 min)
4. Finalize categories and owners (10 min)

## Output Table

| Requirement | Category | Business Outcome | Risk if Deferred | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: MFA login | Must | Security baseline | Compliance risk | Platform Lead |

## Exit Criteria

- Every item has one category.
- Every `Must` has explicit failure rationale.
- At least one `Won't` item is documented for scope control.
