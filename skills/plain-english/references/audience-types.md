# Audience Types

Use this reference to adapt message structure by reader profile.

## Executive Audience

- Decision-focused, short attention window.
- Prefer recommendation first, impact second, risk third.
- Keep to one page when possible.

Suggested structure:

1. Recommendation
2. Business impact
3. Risks and mitigations
4. Decision needed and deadline

## Manager Audience

- Needs plan, ownership, and timeline.
- Expects tradeoffs and resource implications.

Suggested structure:

1. Objective
2. Current state and impact
3. Options/tradeoffs
4. Action plan with owners

## Compliance/Legal Audience

- Needs obligations, evidence, and exposure.
- Prefers precise language and traceable facts.

Suggested structure:

1. Requirement or risk statement
2. Evidence/status
3. Gap and remediation steps
4. Reporting timeline

## Cross-Functional Audience

- Needs context across teams and dependencies.
- Prefers concise shared vocabulary and explicit interfaces.

Suggested structure:

1. Summary of change
2. Team impacts
3. Dependencies and blockers
4. Actions by team
