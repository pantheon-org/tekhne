# Scenario 02: Scholar Evaluation — Literature Review Assessment

## User Prompt

The user has written a literature review on "CRISPR-based therapeutics for rare genetic diseases" as part of a PhD thesis. They want feedback on its comprehensiveness and critical synthesis quality.

Evaluate the literature review focusing on coverage, critical synthesis, gap identification, and source currency. Provide a dimension-level report and prioritised feedback.

## Expected Behavior

1. Prioritise Literature Review, Problem Formulation, and Writing Quality dimensions, weighting them appropriately for a thesis chapter
2. Assess whether the review covers foundational and recent work, including seminal papers
3. Distinguish between mere summarisation and critical synthesis, noting whether themes and contradictions are identified
4. Identify whether the review clearly maps out open research questions and gaps
5. Frame weaknesses as improvement opportunities with specific, actionable suggestions

## Success Criteria

- **Literature review dimensions prioritised**: The agent focuses on Literature Review, Problem Formulation, and Writing Quality dimensions, weighting them appropriately for a thesis chapter.
- **Coverage comprehensiveness assessed**: The agent comments on whether the review covers foundational and recent work, including seminal papers.
- **Critical synthesis evaluated**: The agent distinguishes between mere summarisation and critical synthesis, noting whether themes and contradictions are identified.
- **Research gaps explicitly addressed**: The agent identifies whether the review clearly maps out open research questions.
- **Feedback framed constructively**: Weaknesses are presented as improvement opportunities with specific, actionable suggestions.

## Failure Conditions

- Agent applies generic research paper dimensions without weighting for a literature review thesis chapter
- Agent does not assess coverage of foundational and recent work
- Agent fails to distinguish between summarisation and critical synthesis
- Agent does not address whether the review maps out open research questions
- Agent presents weaknesses negatively without actionable improvement suggestions
